Why Chickens are Awesome

  • They lay delicious eggs
  • They make funny clucking sounds
  • Baby chicks are adorable

Hens

Female chickens are called hens. They lay the eggs!

Roosters

Male chickens are called roosters. They protect the hens.

Chicks

Baby chickens are called chicks. They are super cute and fluffy!
